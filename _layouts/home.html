Charalambos K. Themistocleous
Associate Professor of Speech, Language, and Communication
Department of Special Needs Education
University of Oslo

I am an associate professor at the Department of Special Needs Education, University of Oslo. My research aims to determine the neural mechanisms and cognitive processes underlying speech, language, and communication. I am interested in Human-Centered AI and I am leading the development of "Open Brain AI", a computational platform that aims to support clinicians to study language impairments in the patients.
