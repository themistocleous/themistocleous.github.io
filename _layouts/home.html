About

As of January 2016, I am a postdoctoral researcher in computational linguistics and phonetics in the  Centre for Linguistic Theory and Studies in Probability,  Department of Philosophy, Linguistics and Theory of Science at the University of Gothenburg, Gothenburg Sweden. Previously, I was a visiting scholar at Princeton University, and a temporary lecturer at the University of Cyprus. Please find more information in my Curriculum Vitae.
