Thank you for visiting my website. I am a computational neurolinguist, my research aims to determine the neural mechanisms and cognitive processes underlying speech, language, and communication.


I am interested in Human-Centered AI and I am leading the development of "Open Brain AI", a computational platform that aims to support clinicians to study language impairments in the patients.

OBAI allows you to explore automatic ways to analyze language using AI, automatic speech transcription, and Natural Language Processing.
